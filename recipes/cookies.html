Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a legendary dessert, for good reason. They are so incredibly tasty. The softness of the cookie, along with the explosion of chocolate flavor make it a snack to die for.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Vanilla
  • Brown Sugar
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour and baking soda.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat in salt, vanilla, and eggs until well combined.
  4. Mix in flour mixture until well combined.
  5. Drop balls of dough 2 inches apart onto parchment lined baking sheets.
  6. Bake for about 8-10 minutes.
  7. Enjoy your cookies!!!
